Mission

NextLevel Redmond is run by two Redmond High School students who are passionate about using their skills to help the community around them.

Due to the pandemic, we are doing our best to provide free education over the internet. Students can learn important skills without leaving home.

Education

At NextLevel Redmond, we try to teach skills that put our students on a path fo lifelong success.

We also run a Redmond COVID-19 Recovery Fund for the community, which you can donate to here.
